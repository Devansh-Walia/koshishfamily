Koshish Member's Report

Date wise report

  1. March 22nd

    Dharma was admitted to a local hospital in Patna because his creatinine was 30+ and his Uric acid was also high.
    For three days he was on dialysis because his creatinine level was still fluctuating. He was prescribed ETS dialysis.

  1. March 24th

    He had a minor hand surgery where Fistula was planted for proper blood flow.

  1. March 29th

    He was discharged.

  1. April 1st

    He came to Delhi and visited his doctor Sandeep Guleria who once again informed that a kidney transplant is the only option.
    The same day donor's and the reciver's tests were done and everything seemed good.

  1. April 2nd

    Dharma and donor (his mother) visited the hospital again for some more tests and dialysis. After that he developed high fever of 105. He was then admitted to Apollo Emergency. He got infected due to the dialysis pipe.

  1. April 6th

    He was discharged after treatment of the infection and one more round of dialysis.
    On Monday he is called again to the hospital for further plan of action and treatment.
